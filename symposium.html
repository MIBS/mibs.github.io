2016 Integrative Bioscience Undergraduate Research Symposium

MIBS held their inaugural undergraduate research symposium on April 4th 2016. Talks were held at Thomson House. A variety of food and drinks were served, including pizza! Below is the speakers list:

  • Kevin Luk Introduction + Association of Low-Density Lipoprotein Cholesterol-Related Genetic Variants With Aortic Valve Calcium and Incident Aortic Stenosis
  • Jonathan Fischer-Rush Using morphometrics to predict the Impact of Invasive species in South Africa
  • Angela Liu Circular DNA Diffusion Under Various Nano-confinements
  • Pierre Thodoroff Automatic Epileptic Seizure Detection
  • Christina Chou NMDA receptor mediated calcium supralinearities in pyramidal cell axons of Rim1αβ knockout mice
  • Christina-Marie Boghdady, Johanan Idicula, Vivian Tse Printing Nanodot Lattices to Optimize Cellular Mechanics Assays using Traction Force Microscopy
  • Ariel Greiner Exploring the Extinction Debt in Simulated Metacommunities
  • Joe Su Ancestral Primate IPSC Epigenome Reconstruction
  • Kathy Tang The Role of Paxillin in Adhesion Dynamics
